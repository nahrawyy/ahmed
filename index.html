About: Ahmed Elnahrawy

Ahmed Elnahrawy is a skilled and adaptable professional with a rich and diverse career that spans several fields, showcasing his ability to quickly learn and succeed in different industries. With a foundation in IT support and data entry.
Ahmed began his career at Vodafone (Egy) between 2016 and 2018, where he gained technical skills essential for troubleshooting and managing data.
Afterward, Ahmed transitioned into freelance car sales from 2018 to 2020, where he demonstrated entrepreneurial skills and his ability to connect with clients on a personal level, honing his negotiation and customer service abilities.
From 2020 to 2023, he worked as a Python developer at Smart Solutions (Egy), where he developed backend solutions, showcasing his programming and problem-solving skills. In parallel, Ahmed has been working as a freelance graphic designer since 2018, combining his creative abilities with his technical expertise to deliver high-quality visual designs.
Education:
Ahmed studied Maritime Transportation at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (AAST) from 2018 to 2021, earning a GPA of 2.8. His academic background in this field reflects his interest in complex systems and logistics.
